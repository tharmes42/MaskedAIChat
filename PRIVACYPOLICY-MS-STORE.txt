Privacy Policy for MaskedAIChat
Last Updated: 31.08.2023

## Personal Information

This application may collect and store user's personally identifiable information such as e-mail adresses in order to mask and protect them. These information are not transmitted and only stored locally. However be aware that by using this chat application you may reveal your entered private information and/or your identity to the AI services that are called from this app (e.g. OpenAI, Deepl etc.).

## Technical Information

Limited technical information, such as IP addresses, is automatically sent by your device during the use of the application. However, this information is not used or stored by the application.

## Statistical Information

The application may send a small set of non-identifiable information for statistical information about app usage to Microsoft. This data is strictly used for understanding usage patterns and enhancing user experience.

## Third Parties

This application does make use of third-party services that may collect or use your information (such as OpenAI and Deepl). See the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information. You can avoid transmitting your information to these third-party services by not using the application and/or not entering an API key for these services.
